Mountain Biking Tips

For beginners

1.When on rugged dirt trails with obstacles, keep your pedals level!


2.When in a turn, do not hug the inside, instead continue to the outline of the curve and turn then.


3.Watch this video about trail tips, very useful to learn from experts!
